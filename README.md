## 📖 About The Project

**QANOON AI** is Pakistan's first specialized legal AI assistant designed to make Pakistani law accessible to every citizen. Built with cutting-edge AI technology, it helps users understand their legal rights, get diagnosis of legal problems, draft legal documents, and find relevant case laws - all in **Urdu and English**.

### Why QANOON AI?

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| 🇵🇰 220M+ Pakistanis need legal awareness | AI-powered legal guidance 24/7 |
| 📚 Complex legal jargon | Simple Urdu + English explanations |
| 💰 High lawyer consultation costs | Free basic legal assistance |
| 📄 Hard to find relevant case laws | Smart RAG search + citations |
| 🗣️ Language barrier | Bilingual support (Urdu/English) |

## 🧠 Architecture Overview

QANOON AI follows a modular AI architecture:          

LLM Layer → Handles conversation + reasoning           
RAG Pipeline → Retrieves relevant legal sections         
Vector Database → Stores embeddings of laws & cases        
OCR Module → Extracts text from legal documents      
API Layer → Connects frontend with AI services       
## 🔍 How RAG Works
User asks a legal question             
Query is converted into embeddings           
Vector DB retrieves relevant legal sections        
Context is passed to LLM                            
AI generates accurate, citation-backed response
