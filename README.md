## 📖 About The Project

**QANOON AI** is Pakistan's first specialized legal AI assistant designed to make Pakistani law accessible to every citizen. Built with cutting-edge AI technology, it helps users understand their legal rights, get diagnosis of legal problems, draft legal documents, and find relevant case laws - all in **Urdu and English**.

### Why QANOON AI?

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| 🇵🇰 220M+ Pakistanis need legal awareness | AI-powered legal guidance 24/7 |
| 📚 Complex legal jargon | Simple Urdu + English explanations |
| 💰 High lawyer consultation costs | Free basic legal assistance |
| 📄 Hard to find relevant case laws | Smart RAG search + citations |
| 🗣️ Language barrier | Bilingual support (Urdu/English) |

## ✨ Features

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description | Status |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| 🤖 **Legal Chat** | ChatGPT-style conversational AI for legal queries | ✅ Live |
| 🔍 **RAG System** | Retrieves exact sections from Pakistan Penal Code, Constitution, etc. | ✅ Live |
| 📋 **Legal Diagnosis** | Problem → Diagnosis → Solution workflow | ✅ Live |
| 📝 **Document Drafting** | Auto-generate legal notices, plaints, petitions | ✅ Live |
| 🖼️ **PDF/Image OCR** | Upload scanned legal documents, AI extracts text | ✅ Live |
| 🎙️ **Voice Input** | Speak in Urdu/English, AI understands | ✅ Live |
| ⚖️ **Case Law Search** | Find relevant Supreme Court/High Court judgments | 🚧 Coming |
| 📊 **Legal Analytics** | Track case success probabilities | 🚧 Coming |

### Legal Domains Covered
✅ Pakistan Penal Code (PPC) - Sections 1 to 511
✅ Constitution of Pakistan 1973 - Articles 1 to 280
✅ Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC) 1898
✅ Code of Civil Procedure (CPC) 1908
✅ Qanun-e-Shahadat 1984 (Evidence Act)
✅ Muslim Family Laws Ordinance 1961
✅ Supreme Court Judgments (PLD, SCMR)
✅ High Court Judgments (Lahore, Sindh, Peshawar, Balochistan, ICT)
